Strategic Diagnosis

Diagnosis

The Utility Trap

Users exhibit "Episodic Engagement". They open CRED strictly to pay bills (Utility) and abandon it for the rest of the month (The Void).

Current Strategy

Negative Reinforcement

Retention is driven by Fear (avoiding late fees). This creates relief, not delight. It prevents churn but fails to build habit.

Required Strategy

Positive Reinforcement

Pivot from "Debt Management" to "Wealth Creation". Hook users with daily rewards (Wealth/Status) instead of monthly deadlines.

Secondary Growth Levers

Moving beyond the core: Increasing touchpoints & reducing CAC.

1. Contextual Nudges

The Strategy: Move from "Post-Spend" (Bill Payment) to "Pre-Spend" (Advisory).

Leverage the Account Aggregator (AA) framework to read real-time spend data. Instead of just sending a bill at the end of the month, send "Safe-to-Spend" insights during the month.

Impact

Increases App Opens from 1x/month to 4x/month (Weekly Insights).

Budget Breach

85% of Dining Budget used.

2. Social Staking

The Strategy: Move from "Burning Cash" (CAC) to "Building Clout" (Social Capital).

Wealthy users (HNIs) are less motivated by ₹10 cashback and more by Status. We introduce "CRED Squads"—city-level leaderboards for financial discipline (e.g., "Top 1% Saver in Indiranagar").

Impact

Reduces Rewards Burn by ~15% while increasing retention.
